The Hearing Impaired
Script

The video starts with a close-up shot of Abdi Urgessa, who is deaf and cannot speak or hear. He is seen approaching his dormitory, and as he enters, his dorm mates are having fun. Abdi greets everyone and sits on his bed.

After a few minutes, Abdi's dorm mate, Biniam, asks Amanuel if he has heard about the orientation. Amanuel responds negatively, asking what it is about. Biniam explains that the orientation is mandatory, with attendance being taken, and it is essential to attend since it starts in just five minutes at 4:00LT. Yadessa inquires whether the orientation is for providing tutorials for graduate students, to which Biniam responds that the department head will gather all student IDs and discuss how to be prepared for the final exit exam within the next three months.

Amanuel suggests they should go since there is attendance, and they have only five minutes left. Biniam checks his phone and informs everyone that there are only three minutes left. Amanuel agrees, and Biniam says "yab to yabsira," after which three of Abdi's dorm mates leave for the orientation.

Yabsira takes a Bluetooth speaker, connects it to his phone, and starts playing loud music, dancing to it. He goes to the toilet, but Abdi is unable to hear anything. Another neighbor dorm mate studying nearby hears the music and comes to Abdi's dorm, shouting that they are unable to study because of the noise. Abdi stares at him, not understanding what he is saying. The boy shuts off the music and repeats his concern, but Abdi still can't understand him. Abdi uses sign language to indicate that he doesn't understand, and the boy tries to use sign language to help Abdi understand the issue. They eventually agree, and the boy goes back to his dorm.

The next day, Abdi is walking, and a boy on a bicycle tries to call him to give him space. Abdi doesn't hear him, and the boy stops his bike and asks what Abdi's problem is. On another day, Abdi's mobile phone vibrates while he and his dorm mates are hanging out. He checks his phone and hands it to Biniam, who answers the call. Biniam and Yadessa are shocked to learn that Abdi's close friend is in an emergency. Biniam writes a text on Abdi's mobile, which Abdi reads and becomes frustrated. Biniam sits with Abdi and takes his phone, writing a text suggesting they go to the clinic to check on their friend. They all rush to the clinic, and Biniam and Yadessa take Abdi to his friend's room.
